Mashed Potatoes
Recipe Pictures
  1. Peel, cut and boil potatoes in water with stock cube and salt
  2. Once fork-tender, strain and transfer to bowl
  3. Mash potatoes with potato masher until the big chunks are relatively smaller
  4. Add butter, milk, garlic granules, onion granules, salt and pepper and mash again
  5. Add cheese and stir in
  6. OPTIONAL: Cook and add bacon bits for extra flavour
